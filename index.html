Hey! I'm Kristion, a Computer Science major with a Cybersecurity minor at Kennesaw ('29). This motivated me to address similar disparities in tech, where I found myself among the few black men or people of color in classes & mentorships because of cultural discouragement. Active in NSBE & Colorstack, I enhance my skills and contribute to diversifying tech through mentorship & service. I'm excited to pursue research, internships, & jobs in tech. Join me on my journey towards a more inclusive future in technology!
